About

Daedalus is a website that helps people find concrete solutions to their everyday problems.

Founded in 2023, Daedalus aims at making people's lives easier by offering products that cater to their specific living situation and spending power.

With a diverse team of dedicated workers, Daedalus brings its users a variety of products from which to choose, always aiming to be inclusive of people of different backgrounds and cultural sensitivities.

"Ever since I was little, I have always encountered difficulties in my daily life due to a neurodivergence called ADHD.
Unfortunately, in my country there is still very little awareness of the effects of ADHD in adults, so it has always been difficult for me to find solutions that would help me do all those things that are necessary to live on your own, such as washing the dishes, cleaning the house or even just reminding myself not to skip meals or to brush my teeth regularly.

Precisely for this reason, I thought: "How nice it would have been to consult a website specifically designed to help people with various difficulties find concrete solutions!"
I'm certain if I'd had access to such a site, I could have solved a lot of problems in my life much sooner.

So I decided to create a website that does just that: it helps its users find and buy targeted solutions for their own individual problems.

The site will allow the user to specify their problems: for example, the user could specify that they are unable to wash the dishes regularly; thanks to this information, the site will understand that what said user most likely needs is a dishwasher.
After that, the user can specify the reason why they don't have a dishwasher yet: the user could say, for example, they don't have enough space for a classic dishwasher, therefore the site will suggest some portable mini dishwashers that can also be placed on a table. Or maybe the user will say that they lack the money to buy a classic dishwasher; in that case the site will offer them the cheapest dishwashers as well as the chance of paying in instalments, if they so choose.
Another example could be a user who often ends up skipping meals, perhaps because they don't have enough time to cook, or because they tend to forget about it when too focused on something else. The site will likely offer them a cooker with an integrated timer that starts cooking its content at pre-established times. This would help the user, who would only have to insert the necessary ingredients in the morning and then not have to think about it anymore, because lunch or dinner will be ready at the right time.

Such a site would allow potential buyers to more easily find the products of various companies, as the problems people face are of varied in nature."

I hope you will find Daedalus to be a useful resource to better your own life."
